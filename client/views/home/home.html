KitchenApp

Purpose

The idea behind kitchen app is to allow you to plan meals and shopping. By building meals and then placing them into the calendar, the system will allow you to:

  • plan meals
  • see what you have in your cupboards
  • keep a shopping list

...and you can even add your own recipes and ingredients!

To begin, please register or sign in above...

How to use the app

(Semantically running right to left on the main menu)

  • Ingredients are the raw materials.
  • Recipes are the ideas for a meal.
  • Shopping handles the purchasing of those ingredients.
    • The Shopping List shows you what you need to buy to make your pending meals.
    • The Budget view shows you how well you're doing against your nominated budget.
  • Your Cupboard shows you what you've got in stock and allows you to allocate food to different meals.
  • Meals signify the intent of taking ingredients to follow a recipe.
    • The Build Meals view allows you to Add a Meal:
      • If you have all the ingredients, then those ingredients are removed form your Cupboard and the meal then appears in your Complete Meals listing.
      • If you don't have the ingredients then the meal goes into the Pending Meals section and the ingredients you are missing appear on the Shopping List, whilst the ingredients you have are removed from your Cupboard.
    • The Schedule Meals view allows you to see your upcoming meals and to place Complete Meals onto a food calendar.
    • You can remove a meal from the calendar and the 'Build Meals' view at any time and the ingredients will be released back into your cupboard.
